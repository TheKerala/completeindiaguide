Delhi

India’s capital city – a blend of Mughal heritage, colonial architecture, modern development, and cultural vibrance.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Delhi (Union Territory)
  • Largest City: Delhi
  • Official Languages: Hindi, English
  • Formation: National Capital Territory Act, 1991
  • Nicknames: “Dilwalon Ki Dilli”
  • Famous For: Red Fort, India Gate, Qutub Minar

History & Heritage

Delhi has served as the capital of several empires, including the Mughals and British India, and is home to UNESCO heritage sites.

  • Mughal Legacy: Red Fort, Humayun’s Tomb.
  • British Era: India Gate, Rashtrapati Bhavan.
  • Modern Delhi: A hub of politics and culture.

Geography & Climate

Located in northern India, Delhi experiences extreme summers, cold winters, and a monsoon season.

  • Rivers: Yamuna River flows through Delhi.
  • Climate: Semi-arid with hot summers and chilly winters.

Top Places to Visit

  1. Red Fort: Mughal masterpiece.
  2. Qutub Minar: UNESCO World Heritage site.
  3. India Gate: War memorial.
  4. Lotus Temple: Iconic Baháʼí House of Worship.
  5. Akshardham Temple: Modern architectural wonder.
  6. Chandni Chowk: Historic market and street food hub.

Festivals

  • Independence Day: Grand celebrations at Red Fort.
  • Diwali & Holi: Celebrated with enthusiasm.
  • Qutub Festival: Cultural performances at Qutub Minar.

Cuisine

Delhi’s food culture is a blend of Mughlai, Punjabi, and street food flavors.

  • Famous Dishes: Butter chicken, chole bhature, paranthas.
  • Street Food: Chaat, golgappas, kebabs.

Arts & Culture

  • Classical and folk music events.
  • Theatre, art galleries, and cultural hubs like India Habitat Centre.

Shopping & Markets

  • Chandni Chowk for traditional goods.
  • Connaught Place and Sarojini Nagar for modern shopping.

How to Reach

  • By Air: Indira Gandhi International Airport.
  • By Rail: New Delhi, Old Delhi, Hazrat Nizamuddin stations.
  • By Road: Well-connected highways and inter-state buses.
  • By Metro: Extensive metro network for local transport.

Best Time to Visit

October to March offers pleasant weather for sightseeing.

Sample Itineraries

2 Days – Delhi Highlights

  • Day 1: Red Fort, Chandni Chowk, Jama Masjid, India Gate.
  • Day 2: Qutub Minar, Lotus Temple, Akshardham Temple.
