Lakshadweep

A tropical paradise of coral islands, lagoons, and stunning marine life in the Arabian Sea.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Kavaratti
  • Islands: 36 islands (10 inhabited)
  • Official Languages: Malayalam, English
  • Formation: Became a Union Territory in 1956
  • Nicknames: “Emerald Islands of India”
  • Famous For: Coral reefs, marine tourism, water sports

History & Heritage

Lakshadweep’s history is deeply connected to maritime trade, Arab influence, and South Indian dynasties.

  • Arab Traders: Introduced Islam in the islands.
  • Chera Dynasty: Ancient rulers of the region.
  • Modern Era: Declared a Union Territory in 1956.

Geography & Climate

Lakshadweep comprises atolls, reefs, and islets surrounded by the Arabian Sea.

  • Main Islands: Kavaratti, Agatti, Bangaram, Minicoy.
  • Climate: Tropical; warm and humid year-round.

Top Places to Visit

  1. Kavaratti: Marine aquarium, mosques, water sports.
  2. Agatti Island: Gateway with stunning beaches.
  3. Bangaram Atoll: Diving and luxury resorts.
  4. Minicoy: Lighthouse, cultural heritage.
  5. Kalpeni: Snorkeling and coral reefs.

Activities & Water Sports

  • Scuba diving and snorkeling.
  • Kayaking and glass-bottom boat rides.
  • Fishing, sailing, and water skiing.

Festivals

  • Id-ul-Fitr: Celebrated across the islands.
  • Muharram: Religious observances.
  • Island Tourism Festival: Showcasing local culture.

Cuisine

Lakshadweep’s cuisine highlights coconut, seafood, and Malabar flavors.

  • Specialties: Fish curry, tuna dishes.
  • Drinks: Coconut water and local sweets.

Adventure & Marine Life

  • Coral reef exploration.
  • Deep-sea diving for rich marine biodiversity.
  • Lagoon cruises and eco-tourism.

How to Reach

  • By Air: Agatti Airport (connected via Kochi).
  • By Ship: Passenger ships from Kochi.

Best Time to Visit

October to March is the best time for pleasant weather and water sports.

Sample Itineraries

4 Days – Agatti & Bangaram

  • Day 1: Arrive at Agatti Island.
  • Day 2: Explore beaches & water sports.
  • Day 3: Day trip to Bangaram Atoll.
  • Day 4: Departure.
