Andaman & Nicobar Islands

An archipelago of tropical islands in the Bay of Bengal, known for pristine beaches, marine life, and historical landmarks.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Port Blair
  • Largest City: Port Blair
  • Official Languages: Hindi, English, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, Nicobarese
  • Formation: Union Territory (1956)
  • Nicknames: “Emerald Islands”
  • Borders: Surrounded by the Bay of Bengal and Andaman Sea
  • UNESCO Sites: None, but the Cellular Jail is a National Memorial

History & Heritage

Known for the historic Cellular Jail, the islands were once a colonial penal settlement during British rule.

  • Cellular Jail: Symbol of India's freedom struggle.
  • Tribal Heritage: Home to tribes like Jarwa, Onge, and Sentinelese.

Geography & Climate

Comprising 572 islands, the archipelago is rich in tropical forests, coral reefs, and marine biodiversity.

  • Climate: Tropical, humid summers and mild winters.
  • Special Features: Active volcano on Barren Island.

Top Places to Visit (Handpicked)

  1. Havelock Island: Radhanagar Beach, scuba diving.
  2. Neil Island: Natural bridge and coral reefs.
  3. Ross Island: British-era ruins.
  4. Baratang: Limestone caves and mud volcanoes.
  5. Cellular Jail: Light & Sound Show.
  6. Barren Island: India’s only active volcano.

Festivals of Andaman & Nicobar

  • Island Tourism Festival: Cultural and food celebrations.
  • Subhash Mela: Held in Havelock in honor of Netaji.

Cuisine – What to Eat

The islands offer fresh seafood and coastal dishes with a tropical touch.

  • Staples: Fish curry, coconut-based dishes.
  • Specials: Lobsters, crabs, prawns.

Arts & Culture

  • Handicrafts from shells and coconut.
  • Local tribal dances and traditions.

Adventure & Water Sports

  • Scuba diving and snorkeling at Havelock.
  • Sea walking and parasailing.
  • Glass-bottom boat rides.

How to Reach

  • By Air: Veer Savarkar International Airport (Port Blair).
  • By Sea: Ships from Chennai, Kolkata, and Visakhapatnam.

Best Time to Visit

October to May is best for pleasant weather and water sports.

Sample Itineraries

4 Days – Port Blair & Havelock

  • Day 1: Cellular Jail, Ross Island.
  • Day 2: Havelock – Radhanagar Beach.
  • Day 3: Scuba diving & Neil Island visit.
  • Day 4: Baratang caves.
