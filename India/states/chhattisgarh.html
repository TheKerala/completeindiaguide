Chhattisgarh

The land of ancient temples, vibrant tribal culture, waterfalls, and natural beauty in Central India.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Raipur
  • Largest City: Raipur
  • Official Languages: Hindi, Chhattisgarhi
  • Formation: 1 November 2000
  • Nicknames: “Rice Bowl of India”
  • Borders: Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Telangana, Odisha, Jharkhand, Uttar Pradesh
  • UNESCO Sites: None

History & Heritage

Chhattisgarh has a rich history tied to ancient dynasties, Buddhist sites, and tribal culture. It was carved out of Madhya Pradesh in 2000.

  • Sirpur: An ancient Buddhist site.
  • Temples: Known for Bhoramdeo and Rajiv Lochan temples.
  • Tribal Heritage: Vibrant traditions and customs.

Geography & Climate

Chhattisgarh is part of the Deccan Plateau, with lush forests, rivers, and waterfalls.

  • Rivers: Mahanadi, Indravati, Hasdeo.
  • Climate: Tropical with hot summers and monsoon rains.

Top Places to Visit (Handpicked)

  1. Chitrakote Waterfalls: Known as India’s Niagara.
  2. Bhoramdeo Temple: Stunning architectural site.
  3. Sirpur: Archaeological Buddhist site.
  4. Barnawapara Sanctuary: Wildlife and nature.
  5. Raipur: Capital city attractions.
  6. Kanger Valley: Caves and waterfalls.
  7. Maitri Bagh Zoo: Located in Bhilai.

Festivals of Chhattisgarh

  • Bastar Dussehra: A unique tribal celebration.
  • Rajim Kumbh: Spiritual fair on Mahanadi banks.
  • Pola: Bull worship festival.
  • Madai Festival: Tribal festivity with dance and rituals.

Cuisine – What to Eat

Chhattisgarhi cuisine is simple, nutritious, and dominated by rice-based dishes.

  • Staples: Rice, lentils, and leafy greens.
  • Specials: Chila, Fara, Dubki Kadhi.
  • Sweets: Dehrori, Tilgur.

Arts, Handicrafts & Culture

  • Bamboo and bell metal crafts.
  • Traditional tribal dances like Panthi and Raut Nacha.
  • Terracotta art from Bastar.

Adventure & Wildlife

  • Safari at Indravati Tiger Reserve.
  • Trekking in Kanger Valley National Park.
  • Rafting and boating near waterfalls.

How to Reach

  • By Air: Swami Vivekananda Airport (Raipur).
  • By Rail: Raipur Junction and Bilaspur.
  • By Road: NH 53, NH 30 connect major cities.

Best Time to Visit

October to March is ideal for sightseeing and nature tours.

Sample Itineraries

3 Days – Waterfalls & Temples

  • Day 1: Raipur → Bhoramdeo.
  • Day 2: Chitrakote Waterfalls.
  • Day 3: Sirpur archaeological sites.

5 Days – Bastar Tribal Circuit

  • Day 1: Raipur → Jagdalpur.
  • Day 2: Chitrakote & Tirathgarh falls.
  • Day 3: Kanger Valley caves.
  • Day 4: Bastar markets & crafts.
  • Day 5: Return.

Famous Personalities

  • Abdul Karim Telgi: Renowned personality from Raipur.
  • Habib Tanvir: Theatre personality.
