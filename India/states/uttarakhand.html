Uttarakhand

The “Land of Gods” — known for pilgrimage sites, the Himalayas, yoga, and adventure sports.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Dehradun (Winter), Gairsain (Summer)
  • Largest City: Dehradun
  • Official Languages: Hindi, Sanskrit
  • Formation: 9 November 2000
  • Nicknames: “Devbhoomi” (Land of the Gods)
  • Borders: Himachal Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh, Nepal, Tibet
  • UNESCO Sites: Valley of Flowers National Park, Nanda Devi National Park

History & Heritage

Uttarakhand has a rich history of ancient Hindu traditions, dynasties like the Katyuris and Gorkhas, and British colonial influence.

  • Char Dham: One of the holiest Hindu circuits.
  • Kumaon & Garhwal: Historic regions with distinct cultures.
  • State Formation: Carved out of Uttar Pradesh in 2000.

Geography & Climate

Uttarakhand is dominated by the Himalayan ranges, rivers, and forests.

  • Rivers: Ganga, Yamuna, Alaknanda, Bhagirathi.
  • Climate: Alpine in highlands, subtropical in valleys.

Top Places to Visit (Handpicked)

  1. Rishikesh: Yoga capital of the world.
  2. Haridwar: Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri.
  3. Mussoorie: Popular hill station.
  4. Nainital: Lake city of Kumaon.
  5. Valley of Flowers: UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  6. Jim Corbett National Park: Tiger reserve and safaris.
  7. Badrinath & Kedarnath: Sacred temples.

Major Pilgrimage Sites

  • Kedarnath: One of the Char Dham shrines.
  • Badrinath: Sacred Vishnu temple.
  • Gangotri & Yamunotri: Origin of holy rivers.

Festivals of Uttarakhand

  • Kumbh Mela: Held in Haridwar.
  • Ganga Dussehra: Celebrating the descent of Ganga.
  • Makar Sankranti: Local fairs and rituals.

Cuisine – What to Eat

Traditional Garhwali and Kumaoni dishes dominate the local cuisine.

  • Staples: Madua roti, rice, lentils.
  • Specials: Kafuli, Phaanu, Bal Mithai.

Arts, Handicrafts & Culture

  • Aipan folk art.
  • Woolen handicrafts and Pashmina.
  • Garhwali and Kumaoni folk dances.

Adventure & Wildlife

  • River rafting in Rishikesh.
  • Trekking to Roopkund, Hemkund Sahib.
  • Wildlife safaris in Corbett National Park.

How to Reach

  • By Air: Jolly Grant Airport (Dehradun).
  • By Rail: Dehradun, Haridwar, Kathgodam.
  • By Road: Well-connected via NH to Delhi and UP.

Best Time to Visit

March to June and September to November are ideal.

Sample Itineraries

5 Days – Rishikesh & Haridwar

  • Day 1: Haridwar temples & Ganga Aarti.
  • Day 2: Yoga and rafting in Rishikesh.
  • Day 3: Visit Neelkanth Mahadev.
  • Day 4: Local markets & ashrams.
  • Day 5: Departure.

6 Days – Kumaon Hills

  • Day 1: Nainital sightseeing.
  • Day 2: Boating at Naini Lake.
  • Day 3: Almora visit.
  • Day 4: Kausani tea gardens.
  • Day 5: Ranikhet excursions.
  • Day 6: Return.

Famous Personalities

  • Anil Baluni: Politician.
  • Gaura Devi: Leader of the Chipko Movement.
  • Major Somnath Sharma: Param Vir Chakra awardee.
