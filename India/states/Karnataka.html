Karnataka

Known for its heritage sites, wildlife sanctuaries, technology hubs, and diverse culture, Karnataka is a blend of ancient history and modern development.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Bengaluru
  • Largest City: Bengaluru
  • Official Language: Kannada
  • Formation: 1 November 1956
  • Nicknames: “Silicon Valley of India”
  • Borders: Goa, Maharashtra, Telangana, Andhra Pradesh, Tamil Nadu, Kerala
  • UNESCO Sites: Hampi, Pattadakal

History & Heritage

Karnataka has a glorious past with powerful dynasties like the Chalukyas, Hoysalas, and Vijayanagara Empire leaving a lasting legacy.

  • Hampi: Capital of Vijayanagara Empire and UNESCO site.
  • Mysuru: Kingdom of Wodeyars with royal palaces.
  • Belur & Halebidu: Famous Hoysala temple architecture.

Geography & Climate

Karnataka spans coastal regions, Western Ghats, and Deccan plateau.

  • Rivers: Krishna, Kaveri, Tungabhadra.
  • Climate: Tropical monsoon with mild winters.

Top Places to Visit (Handpicked)

  1. Hampi: Ancient ruins and temples.
  2. Mysuru: Mysore Palace, Chamundi Hills.
  3. Coorg: Coffee plantations and hill stations.
  4. Badami & Pattadakal: Cave temples and UNESCO site.
  5. Jog Falls: India’s second-highest waterfall.
  6. Bengaluru: IT hub with parks and palaces.

Festivals of Karnataka

  • Dasara: Celebrated grandly in Mysuru.
  • Ugadi: New year festival.
  • Kambala: Traditional buffalo race.

Cuisine – What to Eat

Karnataka cuisine ranges from spicy coastal dishes to sweet delicacies.

  • Staples: Rice, ragi, coconut-based curries.
  • Specials: Bisi Bele Bath, Mysore Pak, Neer Dosa.

Arts, Handicrafts & Culture

  • Yakshagana – traditional dance drama.
  • Mysore silk sarees and sandalwood carvings.
  • Carnatic classical music and dance.

Adventure & Wildlife

  • Trekking in Western Ghats.
  • Wildlife spotting in Bandipur & Nagarhole.
  • River rafting in Dandeli.

How to Reach

  • By Air: Kempegowda International Airport (Bengaluru).
  • By Rail: Extensive network with Bengaluru and Mysuru as key hubs.
  • By Road: Well-connected highways and bus services.

Best Time to Visit

October to March is the best season for most destinations.

Sample Itineraries

4 Days – Mysuru & Coorg

  • Day 1: Bengaluru → Mysuru.
  • Day 2: Mysore Palace & Chamundi Hills.
  • Day 3: Coorg – Coffee estates & Abbey Falls.
  • Day 4: Return.

5 Days – Hampi & Badami

  • Day 1: Bengaluru → Hampi.
  • Day 2: Explore Hampi ruins.
  • Day 3: Hampi → Badami.
  • Day 4: Badami & Pattadakal.
  • Day 5: Return.

Famous Personalities

  • Sir M. Visvesvaraya: Engineer and statesman.
  • R.K. Narayan: Renowned author.
  • Dr. Rajkumar: Legendary Kannada actor.
