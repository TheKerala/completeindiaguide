Tripura

The jewel of Northeast India — a fusion of royal palaces, temples, lakes, and tribal culture.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Agartala
  • Largest City: Agartala
  • Official Languages: Bengali, Kokborok, English
  • Formation: 21 January 1972
  • Nicknames: “Queen of Hills”
  • Borders: Bangladesh, Assam, Mizoram
  • UNESCO Sites: None

History & Heritage

Tripura has a royal history influenced by the Manikya kings, Mughal suzerainty, and British rule before joining India in 1949.

  • Manikya Dynasty: Built iconic palaces and temples.
  • Colonial Period: Princely state under British India.
  • Statehood: Achieved on 21 January 1972.

Geography & Climate

Tripura is surrounded by hills, valleys, and rivers with a tropical monsoon climate.

  • Rivers: Gomati, Manu, Khowai.
  • Climate: Warm summers, heavy monsoon, mild winters.

Top Places to Visit (Handpicked)

  1. Ujjayanta Palace: Iconic royal palace and museum.
  2. Neermahal: Water palace in Rudrasagar Lake.
  3. Unakoti: Rock carvings and ancient sculptures.
  4. Jampui Hills: Scenic hill station.
  5. Tripura Sundari Temple: One of 51 Shakti Peethas.
  6. Dumboor Lake: Picturesque lake with boating.
  7. Sepahijala Wildlife Sanctuary: Wildlife and birdwatching.

Royal Palaces & Heritage

  • Ujjayanta Palace: Stunning Indo-European architecture.
  • Neermahal: Palace on the water.
  • Kunjaban Palace: Scenic hilltop palace.

Festivals of Tripura

  • Kharchi Puja: Worship of 14 deities.
  • Durga Puja: Grand celebrations in Agartala.
  • Garia Puja: Tribal festival of prosperity.

Cuisine – What to Eat

Tripuri cuisine features rice, fish, and bamboo shoot dishes.

  • Staples: Rice, fish curries.
  • Specials: Mui Borok (traditional tribal dish), bamboo shoot pickle.

Arts, Handicrafts & Culture

  • Bamboo and cane crafts.
  • Traditional tribal dances like Hojagiri.
  • Handloom weaving (Risa and Pachra).

Adventure & Wildlife

  • Trekking at Jampui Hills.
  • Wildlife spotting in Sepahijala Sanctuary.
  • Boating at Dumboor Lake.

How to Reach

  • By Air: Maharaja Bir Bikram Airport (Agartala).
  • By Rail: Agartala Railway Station.
  • By Road: NH8 connects to Assam and Mizoram.

Best Time to Visit

October to March is best for travel.

Sample Itineraries

3 Days – Agartala & Surrounds

  • Day 1: Ujjayanta Palace & Agartala sightseeing.
  • Day 2: Neermahal & Sepahijala Wildlife Sanctuary.
  • Day 3: Tripura Sundari Temple.

4 Days – Hills & Lakes

  • Day 1: Agartala → Jampui Hills.
  • Day 2: Explore Jampui villages.
  • Day 3: Dumboor Lake & Unakoti.
  • Day 4: Return via Agartala.

Famous Personalities

  • Maharaja Bir Bikram Kishore: Modernizer of Tripura.
  • Dipa Karmakar: Olympic gymnast.
