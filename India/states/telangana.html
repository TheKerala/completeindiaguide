Telangana

The youngest state of India — a vibrant blend of Nizami heritage, temples, IT hubs, and spicy cuisine.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Hyderabad
  • Largest City: Hyderabad
  • Official Language: Telugu (Urdu widely spoken)
  • Formation: 2 June 2014
  • Nicknames: “Land of Nizams”
  • Borders: Maharashtra, Chhattisgarh, Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka
  • UNESCO Sites: Ramappa Temple

History & Heritage

Telangana has a rich history shaped by the Satavahanas, Kakatiyas, and the Nizams of Hyderabad. It officially became India’s 29th state in 2014.

  • Kakatiya Dynasty: Known for temples and forts.
  • Nizami Influence: Charminar and Golconda.
  • Modern Era: IT and biotech hub.

Geography & Climate

Telangana features Deccan Plateau landscapes with rivers, hills, and semi-arid climate.

  • Rivers: Godavari, Krishna.
  • Climate: Hot summers, mild winters, monsoon rains.

Top Places to Visit (Handpicked)

  1. Hyderabad: Charminar, Golconda Fort, Ramoji Film City.
  2. Warangal: Ramappa Temple, Thousand Pillar Temple.
  3. Nagarjuna Sagar: Dam and Buddhist sites.
  4. Nizamabad: Dichpally Ramalayam.
  5. Bhadrachalam: Sri Rama temple.
  6. Basara: Gnana Saraswati Temple.
  7. Khammam: Fort and nature escapes.

Monuments & Heritage

  • Charminar: Iconic structure of Hyderabad.
  • Golconda Fort: Medieval citadel.
  • Ramappa Temple: UNESCO World Heritage site.

Festivals of Telangana

  • Bonalu: Goddess Mahakali festival.
  • Bathukamma: Floral festival.
  • Sankranti: Kite flying and feasts.

Cuisine – What to Eat

Telangana cuisine is spicy with rich Nizami flavors and local dishes.

  • Staples: Jowar roti, spicy curries.
  • Specials: Hyderabadi biryani, haleem, sarva pindi.

Arts, Handicrafts & Culture

  • Pochampally Ikat weaving.
  • Bidri metal craft.
  • Deccani painting and dance forms like Perini.

Adventure & Wildlife

  • Trekking at Ananthagiri Hills.
  • Wildlife at Kawal & Eturunagaram sanctuaries.
  • Boating at Hussain Sagar.

How to Reach

  • By Air: Rajiv Gandhi International Airport (Hyderabad).
  • By Rail: Secunderabad, Hyderabad, Warangal stations.
  • By Road: Well-connected highways and RTC services.

Best Time to Visit

October to March is ideal for exploring.

Sample Itineraries

4 Days – Hyderabad & Surrounds

  • Day 1: Charminar, Mecca Masjid.
  • Day 2: Golconda Fort, Qutb Shahi Tombs.
  • Day 3: Ramoji Film City.
  • Day 4: Hussain Sagar & shopping.

5 Days – Warangal & Temples

  • Day 1: Hyderabad → Warangal.
  • Day 2: Ramappa Temple, Thousand Pillar Temple.
  • Day 3: Explore fort and lakes.
  • Day 4: Basara Temple.
  • Day 5: Return to Hyderabad.

Famous Personalities

  • K. Chandrashekar Rao: First Chief Minister of Telangana.
  • Sania Mirza: Tennis champion.
  • Ram Charan: Film actor.
