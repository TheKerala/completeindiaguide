Tamil Nadu

The “Land of Temples” — a treasure trove of Dravidian architecture, classical arts, and coastal beauty.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Chennai
  • Largest City: Chennai
  • Official Language: Tamil
  • Formation: 26 January 1950 (as Madras State, renamed Tamil Nadu in 1969)
  • Nicknames: “Land of Temples”
  • Borders: Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka, Kerala, Bay of Bengal
  • UNESCO Sites: Great Living Chola Temples, Mahabalipuram

History & Heritage

Tamil Nadu’s history spans over 2000 years with the rise of the Cholas, Pandyas, and Pallavas. The state is renowned for its classical arts and temple architecture.

  • Chola Dynasty: Builders of the Great Living Chola Temples.
  • Pallava Heritage: Mahabalipuram monuments.
  • Colonial Era: British Madras Presidency.

Geography & Climate

Tamil Nadu stretches from the Eastern Ghats to the Bay of Bengal, with fertile plains and coastal regions.

  • Rivers: Kaveri, Vaigai, Palar.
  • Climate: Tropical, with hot summers and northeast monsoons.

Top Places to Visit (Handpicked)

  1. Chennai: Marina Beach, Kapaleeshwarar Temple.
  2. Madurai: Meenakshi Amman Temple.
  3. Thanjavur: Brihadeeswarar Temple.
  4. Mahabalipuram: Shore Temple, rock carvings.
  5. Rameswaram: Ramanathaswamy Temple.
  6. Ooty & Kodaikanal: Hill stations.
  7. Kanyakumari: Vivekananda Rock Memorial.

Major Temples & Spiritual Circuits

  • Meenakshi Temple: Madurai.
  • Brihadeeswarar Temple: Thanjavur.
  • Ramanathaswamy Temple: Rameswaram.

Festivals of Tamil Nadu

  • Pongal: Harvest festival.
  • Chithirai Festival: Madurai celebration.
  • Natyanjali: Dance festival in Chidambaram.

Cuisine – What to Eat

Tamil cuisine is known for its vegetarian delicacies, spices, and rice-based dishes.

  • Staples: Rice, sambar, rasam.
  • Specials: Dosa, idli, pongal, Chettinad dishes.

Arts, Dance & Culture

  • Bharatanatyam classical dance.
  • Kalamkari & Tanjore paintings.
  • Temple architecture and Dravidian heritage.

Adventure & Wildlife

  • Wildlife in Mudumalai & Anamalai.
  • Trekking in Western Ghats.
  • Beaches like Marina & Mahabalipuram.

How to Reach

  • By Air: Chennai International Airport, Madurai, Coimbatore.
  • By Rail: Major railway junctions in Chennai, Madurai, Coimbatore.
  • By Road: Extensive highways and bus networks.

Best Time to Visit

November to March is ideal for sightseeing.

Sample Itineraries

5 Days – Chennai & Mahabalipuram

  • Day 1: Explore Chennai temples and beaches.
  • Day 2: Visit Mahabalipuram monuments.
  • Day 3: Kanchipuram temples.
  • Day 4: Shopping & cultural shows in Chennai.
  • Day 5: Departure.

6 Days – Madurai, Rameswaram & Kanyakumari

  • Day 1: Madurai temple tour.
  • Day 2: Travel to Rameswaram.
  • Day 3: Ramanathaswamy Temple & Dhanushkodi.
  • Day 4: Kanyakumari sightseeing.
  • Day 5: Vivekananda Rock & sunset.
  • Day 6: Return journey.

Famous Personalities

  • C. N. Annadurai: Former Chief Minister.
  • Rukmini Devi Arundale: Bharatanatyam revivalist.
  • A. R. Rahman: Oscar-winning composer.
