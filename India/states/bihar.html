Bihar

The land of ancient universities, Buddhism, Jainism, and vibrant festivals — a blend of history and spirituality.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Patna
  • Largest City: Patna
  • Official Languages: Hindi, Urdu
  • Formation: 22 March 1912
  • Nicknames: “Land of Enlightenment”
  • Borders: Uttar Pradesh, Jharkhand, West Bengal, Nepal
  • UNESCO Sites: Mahabodhi Temple, Nalanda Mahavihara

History & Heritage

Bihar is the cradle of ancient civilizations, home to Magadh, Maurya, and Gupta empires. It is a birthplace of Buddhism and Jainism, and holds immense historical significance.

  • Nalanda University: One of the world’s oldest universities.
  • Patliputra: Ancient capital of Maurya Empire.
  • Bodh Gaya: Where Buddha attained enlightenment.

Geography & Climate

Bihar lies in the fertile Indo-Gangetic plain, divided into North and South Bihar by the Ganga River.

  • Rivers: Ganga, Kosi, Gandak, Son.
  • Climate: Humid subtropical; hot summers, cold winters.

Top Places to Visit (Handpicked)

  1. Bodh Gaya: Mahabodhi Temple, Bodhi Tree.
  2. Nalanda: Ancient Nalanda University ruins.
  3. Rajgir: Hot springs, Vulture’s Peak.
  4. Patna: Golghar, Patna Museum.
  5. Vaishali: Birthplace of Lord Mahavira.
  6. Vikramshila: Ancient Buddhist learning center.
  7. Kesaria: Tallest Buddhist stupa in India.

Festivals of Bihar

  • Chhath Puja: Sun worship festival.
  • Sonepur Mela: Famous cattle fair.
  • Makar Sankranti: Kite flying and celebrations.
  • Sama Chakeva: Mithila cultural festival.

Cuisine – What to Eat

Bihari cuisine is known for its earthy flavors and traditional sweets.

  • Staples: Litti Chokha, rice and dal.
  • Sweets: Khaja, Thekua, Tilkut.
  • Specials: Bihari kebabs, sattu paratha.

Arts, Handicrafts & Culture

  • Madhubani painting (Mithila art).
  • Stone and wood carvings.
  • Traditional folk dances like Jat-Jatin.

Adventure & Wildlife

  • Valmiki Tiger Reserve safari.
  • Nature treks in Rajgir hills.
  • Boating on Ganga ghats.

How to Reach

  • By Air: Jay Prakash Narayan Airport (Patna).
  • By Rail: Patna Junction and other major stations.
  • By Road: NH 31, NH 30 connect Bihar to other states.

Best Time to Visit

October to March is ideal for exploring historical and spiritual sites.

Sample Itineraries

4 Days – Bodh Gaya & Nalanda

  • Day 1: Patna sightseeing.
  • Day 2: Bodh Gaya tour.
  • Day 3: Nalanda & Rajgir.
  • Day 4: Return via Vaishali.

5 Days – Mithila & Cultural Tour

  • Day 1: Patna → Darbhanga.
  • Day 2: Explore Mithila art villages.
  • Day 3: Sonepur Mela / festivals.
  • Day 4: Rajgir hills.
  • Day 5: Return.

Famous Personalities

  • Chanakya: Ancient philosopher and strategist.
  • Jayaprakash Narayan: Freedom fighter and leader.
